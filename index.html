How To Play 📜

Your goal is to memorize and repeat the sequence of colours shown on the grid. Each round, the game will light up a sequence of colours, and your task is to click the same colours in the correct order.

The sequence starts off short and simple, but as you progress, it will become longer and more challenging. If you click the wrong colour, the game ends, and your final score will be displayed. To win, you’ll need sharp focus and a great memory!
